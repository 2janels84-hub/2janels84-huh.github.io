THE LANTERN IN THE STORM

CHARACTERS:
Eli - Eli is a young boy who lives in a small coastal village. He is brave and always helps others, especially the people around him in his community. His bravery is shown through his willingness to care for others and do what is right, even in difficult situations.<

SETTING:
The story takes place in a coastal village near the sea with a lighthouse on a hill. The village is close to the ocean, where ships pass by regularly, and the lighthouse serves as an important guide for sailors traveling near the shore.

CONFLICT:
A strong storm caused the lighthouse light to go out, putting the ships in danger. Without the light, sailors could not clearly see their path, making it difficult for them to navigate safely through the stormy waters.

MORAL LESSON:
Even small acts of courage can save many lives. This shows that simple actions, when done with bravery and good intentions, can make a big difference for others.
